TRANSITIONING THE WORLD TO RENEWABLE POWER

Sword and Stone Capital Management (S&S) is a Queensland based, investor in renewables, focussed on the deployment of technological solutions to enhance renewable energy development.

Now in its third year of operation, S&S have undertaken significant research into a range of storage systems and their viability in order to augment the grid network as independent commercial operations without ongoing subsidy.

S&S is committed to an orderly transition to a sustainable electricity grid, delivering reliable dispatchable renewable energy to meet customer demand while enhancing grid stability.

"Energy Storage is an enabling technology that can smooth and shift energy generation and demand profiles. Cost effective storage has the potential to significantly increase the take up of renewables."

- Australian Renewable Energy Agency (ARENA)
