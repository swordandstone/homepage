S+S is a specialist Renewable Energy Investment Manager, offering our clients optimal returns within an Impact Investing Framework.

Sword + Stone Capital Management was created in order to provide institutional-level funds management within the renewable energy market; combining a unique collaborative collection of top-tier Funds Management and Renewable Energy Specialists.

Bringing together industry experts who each have extensive experience in their respective field, creating a formidable team within this lucrative market segment.

Whilst S+S’s investment focus is inherently sustainable; it also utilises its position in the assets to leave an ongoing positive impact in the community. All its investments are evaluated through the secondary lens of tangible social impact in the regions where we invest.

Our Investment Mandate

S+S is, on behalf of our clients, investing in renewable energy facilities. Our mandated asset targets are:

Facilities requiring an upgrade of existing technology
Strategically
important assets
Distressed organisations or underperforming assets
Existing facilities with expansion potential
Infrastructure to support stable and enhanced generation capacity
