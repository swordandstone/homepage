Potential Energy

THE POWER TO STORE AND MAXIMISE OUTPUT

BATTERY VALUE CREATION MODELLING

Analysis of value creation potential of alternative battery technologies, considering full life cycle cost factors, technical attributes and market or bilateral revenue capture. By modelled specific use cases, S&S identifies optimal long term battery economics and value stacking potential.

We actively monitor various information sources that can help predict energy prices, demands and supply. This information is secured in a data warehouse that allows fast and adaptive modelling and back testing of various battery storage enabled energy trading solutions.

BATTERY SUPPLY, INSTALLATION AND ONGOING ASSET MANAGEMENT

We can source the preferred battery technology for certain key use cases and arrange installation for behind the meter or in front of the meter applications. Battery asset management to ensure ongoing effective operation can also be arranged.

We also track, report and control live battery systems to maximise their utility and profitability.

BATTERY AS A SERVICE

To simplify asset ownership and battery optimisation, we can deliver pre-defined storage solutions for customers where we own and operate the battery. To allow customers to avoid capital outlay for the battery while benefiting from incremental battery value stacking without assuming the operational risk.

We offer installation on the customer site of a battery which is owned and operated by S&S to deliver the required storage services at a set fee, with S&S utilizing the battery to meet broader market needs. These arrangements are developed in partnership with the customer to effectively deliver on the customer's storage requirements while reducing cost of storage by allowing S&S to effectively utilizing the remaining battery capacity.

Battery as a Service provides asset investors an opportunity to invest directly in the transition to renewables by owning storage assets critical to enabling greater penetration of renewable generation and which support increased grid stability.

PREDICTIVE MARKET MODELLING

Using various algorithm-based approaches, we continuously refine our models to maximise battery performance and profitability. With efficient and reliable back testing, we can simulate months or real-time trading.
