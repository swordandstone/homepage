ABOUT US

Sword and Stone Capital Management (S&S) is a Queensland-based, Australian company with extensive experience in electricity markets, renewable energy, manufacturing, electrical installations and services and trading in energy markets worldwide.

S&S is committed to facilitate the acceleration of the world's transition to renewable energy through the development of commercially sustainable, renewable investments. Recognising the perishable state of electricity and the intermittent nature of renewable energy generation, significant storage capacity is seen as a critical enabler to higher levels of renewable penetration.

S&S has investigated electricity market services provided by battery energy storage systems (BESS). S&S has evaluated various BESS solutions and has identified a market proven technology for use in the Australian electricity market to support growth in renewable generation.

What Differentiates Us

  • Deep end to end expertise across the electricity value chain (from technical operation of assets to energy trading)

  • Physical energy trading optimization including Al driven algorithm, Spot Price loss mitigation, trading insights software

  • Powerful network of skills and connections to support execution
